# Business Intelligence Portfolio

A collection of hands-on Business Intelligence coursework completed for **MISY 5360 – Business Intelligence** at Lamar University. The projects move through the full BI stack — from the conceptual foundations of analytical data (OLTP vs. OLAP) all the way to enterprise reporting in **SAP BW**, with practical work in **MS Access, SAS Visual Analytics, Tableau, and Excel** along the way.

> **Author:** MD Hasibur Rahman Murad
> **Course:** MISY 5360 – Business Intelligence · Lamar University
> **Focus:** Data warehousing · OLAP modeling · Data visualization · Enterprise BI

## 📂 Project Index

Each folder contains the deliverable as a **PDF** (viewable directly in your browser) plus original files where relevant.

### 1. [OLTP vs. OLAP](01-oltp-olap/)
Business-case analysis contrasting transactional (OLTP) systems with analytical (OLAP) reporting, using a Global Bike Inc. sales scenario. Explains why raw transactional reports don't support managerial decision-making and how an analytical view solves it.
- 📄 [OLTP vs. OLAP Analysis](01-oltp-olap/A1-OLTP-vs-OLAP-Analysis.pdf)

### 2. [Data Analysis](02-data-analysis/)
Exploratory analysis of sales data — identifying lowest-profit product categories, loss-making products, and top cities by orders and profit.
- 📄 [Product Category Analysis](02-data-analysis/A2-Product-Category-Analysis.pdf)
- 📄 [Sales Data Analysis](02-data-analysis/HW4-Sales-Data-Analysis.pdf)

### 3. [MS Access Databases](03-ms-access/)
Relational database work in Microsoft Access — table design, relationships, and queries against a coffee-store sales dataset.
- 🗄️ [Coffee Store Database](03-ms-access/coffee-store-database.accdb)
- 🗄️ [Relational Database](03-ms-access/A3-relational-database.accdb)

*(`.accdb` files download and open in Microsoft Access.)*

### 4. [SAS Visual Analytics](04-sas/)
A progression of SAS Visual Analytics assignments covering data-item classification (category vs. measure), frequency and aggregation, box-plot distributions, and cross-country profitability analysis.
- 📄 [Data Classification](04-sas/A5-SAS-Data-Classification.pdf)
- 📄 [Visual Analytics – Box Plots](04-sas/A6-SAS-Visual-Analytics.pdf)
- 📄 [Profitability Analysis](04-sas/A9-SAS-Profitability-Analysis.pdf)

### 5. [Tableau](05-tableau/)
Interactive visualizations built on coffee-chain and superstore sales data — profit by product type and market size, sales by region, and a geographic map of sales by state and ZIP code.
- 📄 [Coffee Chain Profit Analysis](05-tableau/A7-Tableau-Coffee-Chain-Profit.pdf)
- 📄 [Sales Geography Analysis](05-tableau/A8-Tableau-Sales-Geography.pdf)

### 6. [Excel](06-excel/)
Business-intelligence analysis performed in Microsoft Excel.
- 📄 [Excel BI Analysis (PDF)](06-excel/A10-Excel-BI-Analysis.pdf)
- 📊 [Excel Workbook (source)](06-excel/A10-Excel-BI-Analysis.xlsx)

### 7. [SAP Business Warehouse (BW)](07-sap-bw/)
Enterprise BI work in SAP BW using the Global Bike Inc. dataset — revenue reporting across products and years, InfoObject and Key Figure design, exception aggregation, material master fields, and mapping a sales DataSource onto a snowflake-schema fact table.
- 📄 [Revenue Reporting](07-sap-bw/A11-SAP-BW-Revenue-Reporting.pdf)
- 📄 [InfoObjects & Key Figures](07-sap-bw/A12-SAP-BW-InfoObjects.pdf)
- 📄 [Material InfoObjects](07-sap-bw/A13-SAP-BW-Material-InfoObjects.pdf)
- 📄 [DataSource & Schema Design](07-sap-bw/A14-SAP-BW-DataSource-Schema.pdf)
